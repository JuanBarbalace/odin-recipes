Caesar salad

Description

A Caesar salad is a green salad of romaine lettuce and croutons dressed with lemon juice,
olive oil, egg, Worcestershire sauce, anchovies, garlic, Dijon mustard, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper.

Ingredients

  • chopped romaine lettuce
  • garlicky croutons
  • creamy dressing made with eggs
  • olive oil
  • lemon
  • parmesan
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • anchovies

Steps

  1. Just stoss them together and eat them
