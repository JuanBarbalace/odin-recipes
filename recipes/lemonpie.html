Lemon pie

Description

Lemon pie and Lemon tart are among the world favourite desserts.
Lemon pie is number one in the USA and Lemon tart or Tarte au citron is in the top three in France.
Both names are sometimes used interchangeably but the two preparations are not strictly the same, although they could be described as first cousins.

Ingredients

  • 5 large egg yolks
  • 1 and 1/3 cups of water
  • 1 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon zest
  • 2 tablespoon of unsalted butter

Meringue

  • 5 large egg white
  • 1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine sugar, cornstarch, flour and salt. Gradually stir in water.
    Cook and stir over medium heat until thickened and bubbly. Reduce heat; cook and stir 2 minutes more. Remove from the heat. Gradually stir 1 cup of hot mixture into egg yolks; return all to saucepan. Bring to boil. Cook and stir for 2 minutes.
    Remove from the heat. Stir in the butter, lemon juice and zest until smooth. Pour into pastry shell.
  2. In a bowl, beat egg whites and salt until stiff (but not dry) peaks form.
    Gradually beat in sugar until soft peaks form. Spread over pie, sealing edges to pastry.
    Bake at 350Â° until meringue is golden, 12-15 minutes. Cool. Store any leftovers in the refrigerator.
